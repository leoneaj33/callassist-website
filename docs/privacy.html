Privacy Policy for CallAssist

Last Updated: February 9, 2026
Effective Date: February 9, 2026

CallAssist ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Name: First and last name for appointment scheduling
  • Email Address: For account communication and support
  • Phone Number: To receive appointment confirmations and callbacks
  • Calendar Events: We access your calendar to find available time slots (only when you grant permission)

Voice Data

  • Voice Input: When you use the voice input feature, your speech is processed by Apple's Speech Recognition service
  • Call Audio: During live calls, audio is transmitted through our AI service provider (Vapi) to facilitate appointment booking
  • Listen-In Feature: When you use the "Listen In" feature, call audio is streamed to you in real-time

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: iOS version, device type (for compatibility)
  • App Usage Data: Features used, crashes, errors (stored locally on device)
  • Call History: Records of appointments booked, call duration, transcripts

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Make phone calls to businesses on your behalf to schedule appointments
  • Find available time slots in your calendar
  • Process minute purchases through Apple's In-App Purchase system
  • Provide you with call transcripts and appointment confirmations
  • Enable the "Listen In" feature during active calls
  • Send you appointment reminders and minute expiration warnings
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our service and fix bugs

3. How We Share Your Information

We share your information in the following circumstances:

Third-Party Service Providers

  • Vapi AI: Processes phone calls and voice data to make appointments on your behalf. See Vapi's privacy policy at vapi.ai/privacy
  • Google Calendar: If you choose to connect Google Calendar, we access your calendar through Google's API. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy
  • Microsoft Calendar: If you choose to connect Microsoft Calendar, we access your calendar through Microsoft's API. See Microsoft's privacy policy at privacy.microsoft.com
  • Apple: In-app purchases are processed through Apple. See Apple's privacy policy at apple.com/legal/privacy

Business Information Shared

When making appointments on your behalf, we share with the business:

  • Your name (as provided in your profile)
  • Your phone number (for callback and confirmation)
  • Your email address (for appointment confirmation)
  • Your availability (time slots you've selected)
  • Service requested (appointment details you provided)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Protect against legal liability

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device:

  • User profiles
  • Minute balance and purchase history
  • Call history and transcripts
  • Calendar connection settings

No Cloud Storage

Important: We do not maintain servers or cloud storage. All your data is stored only on your device. If you delete the app, all data is permanently deleted.

Security Measures

  • Data is encrypted on your device using iOS encryption
  • API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Calendar access requires explicit permission
  • Microphone access requires explicit permission

5. In-App Purchases

When you purchase minute packages:

  • Payment Processing: Handled entirely by Apple through their secure payment system
  • We Never See: Your credit card number, billing address, or payment details
  • We Do Receive: Confirmation of purchase and the package purchased
  • Minute Balance: Stored locally on your device only
  • Expiration: Minutes expire 12 months after purchase date
  • Refunds: Handled through Apple's refund process. See Apple Support

6. Calendar Access

If you grant calendar access:

  • Read-Only Access: We only read your availability, we don't modify events
  • No Storage: Calendar data is not stored; we only check availability in real-time
  • Revocable: You can revoke access anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy → Calendars
  • Provider Choice: You choose between Google Calendar, Microsoft Calendar, or skip calendar integration

7. Microphone and Speech Recognition

Voice Input Feature

  • Uses Apple's on-device Speech Recognition
  • Audio is processed locally when possible
  • No voice recordings are stored
  • Transcribed text is stored only if you save it to an appointment

Listen-In Feature

  • Allows you to hear live calls in real-time
  • Audio is streamed but not recorded by us
  • You control when to start/stop listening
  • Business may record calls (depends on their policy)

8. Children's Privacy

CallAssist is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • Edit Profile: Update your name, email, phone number in Settings
  • Delete Data: Delete the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Revoke Permissions: Manage calendar and microphone access in iOS Settings
  • Disconnect Calendar: Unlink calendar in app Settings

Data Portability

Your data is stored in standard formats on your device. Call history and transcripts can be exported (future feature).

Do Not Sell

We do not sell your personal information. We never have and never will.

10. Data Retention

  • While App Installed: Data is retained on your device
  • When App Deleted: All data is permanently deleted
  • Minute Purchases: Purchase history is retained for 12 months (duration of minute validity)
  • Call Transcripts: Retained indefinitely until you delete them

11. International Users

CallAssist is designed for users in the United States. If you use our service from outside the US:

  • Your information may be transferred to and processed in the US
  • Phone calls may incur international charges
  • Calendar integration availability may vary

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Posting the new policy in the app and on this website
  • Sending an in-app notification for material changes

Your continued use of CallAssist after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: What personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: We don't sell personal information, so no opt-out needed
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We won't discriminate for exercising privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.
