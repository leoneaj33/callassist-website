Q: How do I buy minutes?

1. Open the CallAssist app

2. Tap the minute balance widget in the top right corner

3. Choose a package (25, 100, 250, or 500 minutes)

4. Complete the purchase through Apple Pay

5. Minutes are added instantly to your balance

Q: When do minutes expire?

Minutes expire exactly 12 months from the date of purchase.

Example: Purchase on March 1, 2026 ‚Üí Expires March 1, 2027

You'll receive a warning 30 days before expiration. Expired minutes cannot be used and are non-refundable.

Q: How much do calls cost?

Calls cost the actual duration in minutes:

  • Most appointment calls: 3-5 minutes
  • Simple bookings: 2-3 minutes
  • Complex requests: 5-10 minutes

You're only charged for connected time. Failed calls under 30 seconds are free.

Q: How do I link my calendar?

On First Launch:

You'll be prompted to connect Google Calendar or Microsoft Calendar during setup.

To Change Later:

  1. Open Settings tab
  2. Scroll to "Calendar" section
  3. Tap "Change Calendar Provider"
  4. Choose Google, Microsoft, or skip
Q: Can I get a refund?

Refund requests must be submitted through Apple:

  1. Visit: Apple Support - Request a Refund
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find your CallAssist purchase
  4. Select "Request a Refund"

Apple reviews refund requests on a case-by-case basis. Unused minutes are generally non-refundable per our Terms of Service.

Q: What is the "Listen In" feature?

Listen In lets you hear the appointment call in real-time:

  1. When placing a call, enable "Listen In"
  2. Once the call connects, you'll hear the live audio
  3. You can transfer the call to yourself if needed
  4. You can also end the call early

Note: Listening uses data. WiFi recommended for best quality.

Q: How do I use voice input?

Voice input lets you describe appointments by speaking:

  1. On the "New Call" screen, tap the microphone icon
  2. Grant microphone permission (first time only)
  3. Speak your appointment details
  4. Text appears automatically in the description field
  5. Tap microphone again to stop recording
Q: What if the AI doesn't understand me?

If voice input has trouble:

  • Speak clearly and at normal pace
  • Reduce background noise
  • Use punctuation words ("period", "comma")
  • Or just type your request manually
Q: Can I make multiple appointments at once?

Yes! You can queue multiple appointment calls:

  • Each call is made sequentially (not simultaneously)
  • Check your History tab to track all calls
  • Each call deducts minutes separately
Q: What happens to my data if I delete the app?

All data is permanently deleted:

  • User profile
  • Minute balance (unused minutes lost!)
  • Call history and transcripts
  • Calendar connections

Before deleting: Export any important transcripts or information you want to keep.

Q: How do I add another profile?

You can create multiple profiles for different people or use cases:

  1. Open Settings tab
  2. Scroll to "Active Profile" section
  3. Tap "Add Another Profile"
  4. Enter name, email, phone for new profile
  5. Switch between profiles anytime in Settings

Note: All profiles share the same minute balance.

üîß Troubleshooting

Problem: Minutes not showing after purchase

Try these steps:

  1. Close and reopen the app
  2. Check App Store for pending purchases
  3. Tap "Restore Purchases" in the Buy Minutes screen
  4. If still missing after 1 hour, contact support with your receipt
Problem: Calendar won't connect

For Google Calendar:

  1. Make sure you're signed in to Google on your device
  2. Allow CallAssist to access your Google account
  3. Grant calendar permissions when prompted

For Microsoft Calendar:

  1. Make sure you're signed in to Microsoft on your device
  2. Allow CallAssist to access your Microsoft account
  3. Grant calendar permissions when prompted
Problem: Call failed or error during call

Common causes:

  • Poor internet connection (check WiFi/cellular)
  • Business phone number unreachable
  • Business closed or not answering
  • Incorrect phone number format

You're only charged if the call connects successfully. Failed calls under 30 seconds are free.

Problem: Listen In audio not working

Try these steps:

  1. Check your internet connection (WiFi recommended)
  2. Ensure microphone permissions are granted
  3. Turn up device volume
  4. Close other apps using audio
  5. Restart the app and try again
